A little about us

When the people of St. Peters crave a fresh, delicious meal, they come to Hibachi Boy. Since 2014, we've made a name for ourselves by serving up handcrafted sushi and hibachi cuisine cooked fresh when you order. Whether you call in for take-out or you dine with us in our clean and inviting cafe, you can expect the same fantastic service from our friendly team.

Join us on a journey through a culture full of flavor and experience Japan through our food
